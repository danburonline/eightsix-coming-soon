Eightsix Science

Engineering neural tissue for brain repair. We're a team of neuroengineers and neuroscientists with strong AI backgrounds. Our goal is to gradually replace damaged or aged neural tissue with engineered grafts, preserving function throughout the transition.

We work end-to-end: from generative AI and biophysical simulations, through in vitro biofabrication, to surgical validation and procedure design. Our platform takes patient brain scans as input, generates cellular-resolution replacement tissue designs, and outputs fabrication instructions. Target applications include tumour resection cavities, stroke lesions, and traumatic brain injuries where neural tissue has been lost.

The core system combines generative AI models trained on digitised neurons with physics-based growth simulations. The AI generates scaffold architectures. A biophysics simulator predicts how cells will migrate, differentiate, and form connections over weeks. We validate predictions by fabricating tissue and testing integration in animal models.
